### What you NEED

- Practice Pad and Sticks

- A metronome (app or physical)

- A folder for music

- Consistent transportation

- A whole lot of food and water

- A good attitude!

### Other helpful tools

- A Nice Practice Pad
- A Tenor Pad (for tenor players only)
- Heavy Practice Sticks
- Music stand/drum stand
